### Priority Tags
* `[BLOCKER]` - Critical issue preventing progress or breaking production.
* `[HIGH]`    - Important feature or bug fix needed for next release.
* `[MED]`     - Standard development task.
* `[LOW]`     - Nice-to-have, or technical debt repayment.
